Chicken Slop

Ingredients

  • 1 package of chicken breast
  • 1.5 cups of rice
  • 1.5 cups of water
  • 1 Can of unsalted corn
  • 1 Can of Black Beans

Steps

  1. place all ingredients in InstantPot
  2. Turn InstantPot on high pressure mode for 22 minutes
  3. Let InstantPot hold for 10 minutes after 22 minutes and then release pressure
  4. Serve!
