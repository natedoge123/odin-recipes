Ground Beef Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 package of ground beef
  • 1 bell pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 cup of pasta
  • Pasta Sauce

Steps

  1. Fill pot with water and add salt. Start heating to boil
  2. Cut up bell peppper and put in pan
  3. Place ground beef in pan and add the spices you want
  4. Once water is boiling place pasta and allow to cook until "hard to the teeth"
  5. Cook ground beef until brown and then remove from heat
  6. Serve with your favorite suace and enjoy!
